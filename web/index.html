What it is

Three layers. Each does one job well. The daemon stitches them.

🧠 Skills

Disciplines the model applies to its own work. How to think, not what to know. Each ≤150 lines, ≤8 KB.
24 core6 opt

🤖 Agents

Roles the orchestrator can dispatch. Critic, planner — each with a model tier and tool allowlist.
2 agents

⚙️ Orchestrator

Local daemon that ranks, dispatches, executes, logs, and judges every run. FastAPI, SQLite, lazy-started.
