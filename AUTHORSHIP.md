# Authorship

Every skill, agent, and other authored artifact in the Agent Foundry catalog is **original work by the Agent Foundry Contributors**, released under the MIT license.

The catalog is a curated, synthetic body of engineering discipline. Skills are descriptions of methodology, not derived from any single upstream source. Agents are role definitions written for this project's orchestrator.

The private triage ledgers in `catalog/skills.csv` (the under-the-hood tracking of where patterns came from during initial research) are intentionally not surfaced in this repository. They are a research artifact, not a derivation record.

If, after a good-faith read of any specific skill or agent, you believe it contains copied expression from a third-party work, please open an issue titled `AUTHORS REVIEW` and we will replace that material with original language.
